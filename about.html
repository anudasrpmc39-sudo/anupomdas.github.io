About Me

Anupom Das is a medical doctor from Bangladesh with experineces in Public Health Research, Health Promotion, and Health System Strengthening. He graduated in Medicine (MBBS/MD) from Rangpur Medical College under the University of Rajshahi in 2015 and worked in several research projects including clinical trials and disease surveillance. He worked for Bangladesh Civil Services as a Civil Servent since 2020

Currently he is pursuing a Joint Master's degree in Health Informatics at Karolinska Institutet in Sweden. The program is a joint masters program of Karolinska Institute offering MMedSc in Medical Science and Stockholm University offering MSc in Computer and System Science. He is an awardee of Swedish Institute Scholarship for Global Professionals (2025-2027), popularly known as, SI Scholarship.
