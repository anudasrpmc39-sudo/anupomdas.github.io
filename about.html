About Me

Anupom Das is a medical doctor from Bangladesh with experience in Public Health Research, Health Promotion, and Health System Strengthening. He graduated in Medicine (MBBS/MD) from Rangpur Medical College under the University of Rajshahi in 2015 and worked in several research projects including clinical trials and disease surveillance.

He has been serving in Bangladesh Civil Service since 2020. Currently, he is pursuing a Joint Master's degree in Health Informatics at Karolinska Institutet in Sweden. The program is jointly offered by Karolinska Institutet (MMedSc in Medical Science) and Stockholm University (MSc in Computer and System Science). He is an awardee of the Swedish Institute Scholarship for Global Professionals (2025–2027).
